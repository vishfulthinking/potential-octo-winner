# Product Marketing Context

This skill ensures that all AI-generated copy, product features, and UI/UX decisions align with our core marketing context.

## 1. Target Audience
- E-commerce brand owners
- Dropshipping team managers
- Agency founders looking to scale their talent pool

## 2. Pain Points
- Hiring takes too long and requires manual filtering of thousands of resumes.
- Hard to find candidates who have specific e-commerce tool experience (e.g., Shopify, Klaviyo, Gorgias).
- Upwork and Fiverr are too generic and often result in poor-quality hires for niche dropshipping roles.

## 3. Value Proposition
- "Find the perfect e-commerce operator in seconds, not weeks."
- Instant, AI-powered matchmaking over a curated pool of 4,000,000 pre-scraped candidates.

## 4. Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Professional, fast-paced, authoritative, but accessible.
- **Vibe:** Premium SaaS (think $50-$200/mo B2B tool). We value speed and high ROI.

## Instructions for the Agent
Whenever you write copy for the landing page, design a UI flow, or write transactional emails, you MUST review these guidelines to ensure the messaging resonates with dropshippers and agency founders.
